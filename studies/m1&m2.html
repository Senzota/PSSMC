Chatter

Uncontrolled regenerative chatter: each tool pass leaves a wavy surface that disturbs the next cut. The feedback loop grows unstable — vibration amplitude increases with every revolution. In practice this destroys surface finish and accelerates tool wear.

PSSMC

The Predictive Sliding-Surface Model-based Controller measures the state vector x = [y₁, ẏ₁, y₂, ẏ₂]ᵀ and applies an actuator force that exactly cancels the regenerative term, keeping the effective chip thickness at the nominal feed f_z.

Parameters

Tool mass m₁ = 0.2 kg · k₁ = 2.2 × 10⁹ N/m · c₁ = 70 kg/s. Workpiece m₂ = 3.0 kg · k₂ = 1.7 × 10⁸ N/m · c₂ = 290 kg/s. Specific cutting resistance Kc = 1500 N/mm². Axial depth aₚ = 0.5 mm.
